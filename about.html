Web Designer, Wake Surfer, Creator of Start Bootstrap

I'm a web designer pixel pushing in Orlando, Florida. I've been a web designer for over nine years, starting out as a designer for the University of Central Florida.

In 2013, after finishing graduate school, I started a side project called Start Bootstrap - which I now manage professionally alongside the occasional development contract.

My educational background is in entrepreneurship, and I received an MBA with a focus on Technology Ventures from the University of Central Florida in 2012.

When I'm not staring at a computer screen, you'll most likely find me with some sort of board under my feet while on the water, snow, or concrete.
